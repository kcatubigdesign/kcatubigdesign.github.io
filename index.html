About me

I'm a designer based in the New York metro area with 7+ years of experience shaping brands across agencies, SaaS, fintech, and nonprofits. I enjoy turning complex ideas into clear, intentional experiences, whether that means building a visual system, launching a campaign, or creating something people actually connect with.

When I'm not designing, I'm out exploring with my dog Kida. She's my reason for early roadtrips over flights, spontaneous hikes, and generally never sitting still. That same restlessness carries into how I think: I'm drawn to puzzles, systems, and finding the elegant solution hiding underneath a messy problem.

If you're interested in learning more about how I translate my experiences into my work, check me out!
